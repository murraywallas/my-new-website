PROJECT: Mansa Cava, a wine e-commerce site
ROLE: Design and update a website for wine e-commerce.
DURATION: 1-2 months

Vision

Mansa Cava was born as a start-up looking to help small winemakers to sell their products all across Argentina. Throughout time, more wineries and bigger companies presented their wines on the website.

Challenges

  • Competitors offering similar products on the internet.
  • Updating wines and prices monthly
  • Offering personalized customer service within the platform and during the entire purchase experience.
  • Growth through social media and advertising

User Journey

I investigated about other companies in the same province and in the country, to check their purchase funnel and obtaining a better procedure for ourselves.

Guiding questions

  • Who could use this site?
  • What does the user want to buy?
  • How does the user reach us in case of a question?
  • What information should be displayed in each product?
  • How could be maintain a good communication in every step of the purchase?
  • How will we know if the user got a good experience?
